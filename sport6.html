Star Sports Pro Kabaddi is an android app designed for the ultimate Kabaddi enthusiast. If you love the game of Kabaddi and have been following the Pro Kabaddi League then this app is for you. It has a lot of goodies to offer and every Kabaddi lover should definitely have it on their Smartphone.

The Pro Kabaddi League, currently known as Vivo Pro Kabaddi League for sponsorship purpose, is a professional-level kabaddi league in India. It was launched in 2014 and is broadcast on Star Sports. The league's inception was influenced by the popularity of the kabaddi tournament at the 2006 Asian Games.
